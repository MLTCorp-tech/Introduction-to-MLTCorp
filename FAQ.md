# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## 1. What is MLTCorp?
MLTCorp is a technology company focused on providing innovative solutions in machine learning and AI. Our goal is to simplify complex data through advanced analytics.

## 2. Who can I go to for help during onboarding?
You can reach out to your assigned buddy or the HR team for assistance. Additionally, check the internal communication channels for immediate help.

## 3. What tools and software will I need?
You will primarily use GitHub, JIRA, and our proprietary ML tools. Ensure you have access to these before starting your projects.

## 4. Where can I find the company policies?
Company policies are available in the internal wiki. Please review them during your first week.

## 5. How can I give feedback?
We encourage feedback through our bi-weekly meetings and surveys sent out at regular intervals. Your opinion matters!

## 6. What is the process for requesting time off?
Time off requests should be made through the HR portal. Ensure you submit your requests in advance to allow for proper planning.

## 7. What are the working hours?
Our standard working hours are from 9 AM to 5 PM (UTC). However, you can discuss flexible hours with your manager if required.

## 8. How do I access training programs?
Training programs can be accessed through our learning management system. New team members are encouraged to participate in at least one program in their first month.

For any other questions, feel free to reach out to your team lead or HR!
